About Me

Get to know the creative behind the work

I'm Bzya Rai, a multidisciplinary content creator with a passion for visual storytelling. With over 5 years of experience across YouTube, photography, video editing, and journalism, I specialize in creating compelling narratives that resonate with audiences.

My journey began in 2018 when I started documenting local stories in my community. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a professional career as my work gained recognition for its authentic perspective and technical excellence.

Today, I work with brands, publications, and individuals to create content that informs, entertains, and inspires. My approach combines journalistic integrity with creative vision to produce work that stands out in today's crowded media landscape.
